FAQ

General questions

What is Reshare?

Reshare is a peer-to-peer lending platform through which members of a community can safely and efficiently share resources. Lenders are able to offer excess goods to their community through the mobile app, and potential borrowers are able to see items available near their location.
Reshare seeks to make the lending process as simple as possible in order to contribute to the benefits of a sharing economy:
  • Respond to climate change by utilizing excess/unused resources and lowering total demand
  • Continue to care for the environment by accelerating sustainable consumption
  • Build relationships within communities and encouraging collaboration
  • Lowering the relative cost of goods by perfectly managing their use
  • Provide access to goods that would otherwise be prohibited economically or logistically

When is the official release?

Reshare will begin testing in select markets in Q1 2017.

What markets will reshare be available in?

New markets will be available based on demand. Please sign up for our newsletter for updates!
Lending

What items can be listed?

Approved item categories is subject to change based on location, and categories will continue to expand.

What is the item lending process?

As a lender, you will meet with the borrower to lend out an item and receive it back.

How are payments handled?

Payments are made to your linked debit card on a per lending transaction basis.

What security is available to me?

Reshare uses the forefront of industry standard fraud detection. Borrowers are identified by a linked credit card as well as a verification process. Damages are taken care of through the app, and a support staff is standing by in the case of any other issue.
Borrowing

What items are available to borrow?

Approved item categories is subject to change based on location, and categories will continue to expand.

What payment options are available?

The only available payment method is a linked credit card.

Can I have the item delivered or other add on services?

Lenders have the option of adding add on services to a listing. These options will be available for you to choose when an item is added to your cart. Item can also be searched for by add on service
